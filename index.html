How to Request a Game Assignment

  • 1. Browse the open slots below, grouped by venue. Each card shows the matchup, time, field, age group, role needed, and fee.
  • 2. Select the game(s) you'd like by clicking the card(s) — you can pick more than one.
  • 3. Enter your name and email in the bar at the bottom, then click Request Assignment(s).
  • 4. This will open a pre-filled email from your own email account to the assignor. Your information is not stored or shared online — it only appears in the email you send.
  • 5. All confirmed assignments are entered into Squadi.

⚠ Assignment is not guaranteed — games may already have been covered by the time your request is received. I will do my best to stay on top of requests and communicate back to you as quickly as possible.
